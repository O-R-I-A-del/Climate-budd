# 🌍 Climate Budd - Your Daily Climate Action Companion

Welcome to **Climate Budd**, an interactive web platform designed to inspire and motivate everyone to become a climate champion! With daily fun facts, actionable challenges, and motivational tips, Climate Budd makes climate action engaging, achievable, and rewarding.

## 🎯 Features

### 📚 Daily Climate Facts
- Get a fresh climate fact every day to stay informed
- Learn about renewable energy, sustainability, and environmental impact
- Share facts with friends and family to spread awareness
- Discover new facts on demand with the "Next Fact" button

### ⚡ Daily Challenges
Climate Budd offers 6 daily challenges to help you take action:
- **♻️ Reduce Waste** - Use reusable products and save 167 plastic bottles per year
- **🚴 Active Transport** - Walk, bike, or use public transit for zero-emission travel
- **💡 Save Energy** - Turn off lights and reduce energy consumption
- **🌱 Plant Connection** - Water plants and connect with nature
- **🌿 Eco Food Choice** - Try plant-based meals and reduce food carbon footprint
- **💬 Spread Awareness** - Share climate action with your community

Complete challenges to:
- Earn Climate Champion status
- Track your CO₂ savings
- Inspire others in your community

### 💡 Climate Action Tips
6 evidence-based tips to maximize your climate impact:
1. **Home Energy** - LED bulbs save 75% energy
2. **Water Conservation** - Save 2,700 gallons annually with shorter showers
3. **Food Waste** - Reduce the 1/3 of food that's wasted annually
4. **Shopping Smart** - Buy second-hand to reduce production emissions
5. **Transportation** - Carpool to reduce CO₂ by 67%
6. **Digital Footprint** - Unsubscribe from unnecessary emails

### 🏆 Champions Leaderboard
- Track your progress with detailed statistics
- See how many actions you've completed
- Monitor your CO₂ savings
- Discover how many people you've inspired
- Climb the ranks: Climate Starter → Earth Protector → Green Guardian → Climate Warrior

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- No installation or accounts required!

### How to Use

1. **Visit the website** - Open `index.html` in your browser
2. **Read today's fact** - Start with the daily climate fact at the top
3. **Complete challenges** - Click "Mark Complete" on the challenges you accomplish
4. **Track progress** - Check the Champions Leaderboard to see your impact
5. **Learn tips** - Read climate action tips to maximize your positive impact
6. **Share** - Share facts with friends to multiply your impact

## 📊 How It Works

Climate Budd uses browser **localStorage** to track your progress:
- Your daily challenges are saved locally
- Progress persists even after closing the browser
- Statistics update in real-time as you complete actions
- Challenge completion records prevent double-counting
