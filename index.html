⚡ Today's East Africa Challenges

♻️

Reduce Plastic Waste

Use cloth bags at the market. Protect wildlife from plastic pollution! 🦁

🚴

Sustainable Transport

Walk, bike, or use public matatu. Save money & reduce emissions!

💡

Save Energy

Turn off lights & unplug devices. Prepare for Uganda's sustainable energy future!

🌱

Plant Native Trees

Plant a tree or help in a community garden. Fight deforestation! 🌳

🌿

Eco Food Choice

Try one plant-based meal. Support local sustainable farming! 🥬

💧

Water Conservation

Collect rainwater or take shorter showers. Protect Lake Victoria! 💙
